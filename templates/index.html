About Us

Our idea is to create "FilmForYou", a smart recommendation system designed to help you find the perfect movies and web series based on your personal tastes.

Using a friendly chatbot, "FilmForYou" asks 10-15 quick and enjoyable questions about your favorite genres, actors, storylines, themes, and languages. With this information, it delivers a customized list of movies and series you’re sure to love.

Our goal is to make your movie-watching experience easy and enjoyable. "FilmForYou" will provide fast, accurate recommendations tailored to your preferences, so you’ll never waste time searching or miss out on great content.
